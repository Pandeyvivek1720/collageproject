about us


ITS Engineering College keeps in mind the ever changing technical skills requirement for industries in India and abroad. Our teaching methods are designed to ensure technology based learning which is in addition to the domain knowledge of the chosen engineering branch. Students get hands on experience through internships in core companies in their chosen branches, experiential learning through industry visits, live projects, expert talks, MOOCs, workshops, case studies, upscale labs, and virtual classroom sessions. Our faculty members keep themselves abreast with the current industry developments and make substantial contribution to the academia through quality teaching, patent publications, seminars and conferences etc. Our constant efforts have made us the top engineering college in Greater Noida and one of the best engineering colleges in AKTU. AICTE NOTIFICATION

as per notification of all indian council for technical education(AICTE)25.03.2009 in connection with prevention and prohibition of rangging in technical institutes,universities deemed to be universities imparting technical education,notify that:all the concerned are requested to make note that rangging totaly banned and anyone found guilty of ragging and orabetting ragging is liable to be punished up state act against ragging.
up prohibition ragging act 1999 which is in effect from 15 may 1999 has the following provision for action against ragging.
(a). ragging within or outside of an education institute is prohibited.
(b). whosoever directly or indirectly commits, participates in abets,or propagates ragging with in or outside any educational institute shall,on be punished with imprisonment for a term up to 2 years and or penalty which may extand to ten thousand rupees.
(c). any student convicted to an offence of raggingshall be dismissed from education for 5 year from the date of order of such dismissal.
(d).whenever any student or as the case may be ,the parent of gurdian or a teacher of any educational institute complaint in writing of ragging to the head of the educational institute,the head educational institute shall without prejudice to the for going previous within seven days of the receipt of the complaint.where on enquiry by the head of the educational institute,it is found that there is no substance,prime facia in the complaint receved,he/she shall intimate the fact in writing to the complaint the decision of the head of the educational institute shall be final.
